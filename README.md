 Shopify E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

##  The Problem
E-commerce businesses often struggle to answer critical questions in real time:
1. Which products are actually driving revenue — and which are underperforming?
2. Which countries are our strongest markets?
3. Why did sales spike in April but crash in May — and how do we prevent that?
4. Are we profitable, or just busy?

Without a centralized view, business owners waste hours digging through spreadsheets,
missing patterns, and making decisions based on gut feeling instead of data.

##  The Solution
This interactive Power BI dashboard gives Shopify store owners and analysts
a single source of truth to monitor, analyze, and act on sales performance —
in seconds, not hours.

## ✨ Key Features
 📈 **Monthly Revenue Trend** — Spot seasonality and sales dips instantly
 🌍 **Sales by Country** — Know exactly where your customers are coming from
 🏆 **Top 5 Products by Revenue** — Double down on what's working
 🔢 **KPI Cards** — Revenue, Units Sold, Orders, and Profit at a glance
 🎛️ **Dynamic Filters** — Slice by Month, Country, and Product in real time

## 💡 Business Insights Uncovered
- 🚨 Revenue **dropped 68% from April to May** — signals a need for post-peak retention strategy
- 🏅 **Smart Fitness Watch** alone drives ~34% of total revenue
- 🌍 **USA and UK** are the top 2 markets — ideal targets for paid ad campaigns
- 📦 Low order count (95) vs units sold (196) means customers are buying **multiple items per order** a bundling opportunity

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop** — Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive visuals
- **Shopify API** — Connected directly to live store data via REST API
- **Power Query** — API data extraction, transformation and loading (ETL)
- **Map Visual** — Microsoft Bing Maps integration
